What Is "Indigo La End"?

Indigo la End is a Japanese indie-rock band led by vocalist and songwriter Enon Kawatani.
They are considered to one of the most successful bands in the "Indie-Rock" genre in japan.
The band's name was inspired by Japanese band "Spitz" and their album Indigo Chiheisen.
